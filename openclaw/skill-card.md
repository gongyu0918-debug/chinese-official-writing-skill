## Description: <br>
Primarily drafts, revises, compresses, and reviews Chinese official documents and formal workplace materials, including 通知、请示、报告、函、复函、批复、意见、方案、说明、申请、征求意见函、采购公告、可研、调研、总结、工作要点、审查材料、讲话稿、述职报告. Explicit Chinese undergraduate, master's, and course paper tasks enter a separate on-demand leaf and do not share the official-document workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this skill primarily to produce or review Chinese formal documents through genre, handling-element, structure, and tone checks. Its on-demand paper leaf remains secondary in product presentation, source-bounded, and subject to qualified academic review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated official, procurement, legal, financial, audit, academic, or sign-off language may contain incorrect facts, dates, authorities, costs, sources, citations, or approval wording. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as draft assistance and have a qualified person verify facts, dates, authorities, costs, sources, citations, methods, and final approval language before use. <br>
Risk: Drafts may include unsupported estimates, missing business details, or unresolved placeholders if the user has not supplied complete source information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep missing data outside the final body for user confirmation, and use the included review checklist or prose-lint script where appropriate before relying on the text. <br>
