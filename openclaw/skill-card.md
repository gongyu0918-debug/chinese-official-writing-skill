## Description: <br>
Drafts, revises, compresses, and reviews Chinese official documents and formal workplace materials, including notices, requests, reports, letters, replies, approvals, plans, research summaries, speeches, procurement notices, and related review tasks. It covers 通知、请示、报告、函、复函、批复、意见、方案、说明、申请、征求意见函、采购公告、可研、调研、总结、工作要点、审查材料、讲话稿、述职报告 and similar Chinese formal texts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this skill to produce or review Chinese formal documents with better genre selection, document structure, handling-element checks, official tone, and reduced AI-like phrasing. It supports drafting and review workflows but keeps legal, financial, procurement, audit, and final sign-off conclusions subject to qualified human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated official, procurement, legal, financial, audit, or sign-off language may contain incorrect facts, dates, authorities, costs, or approval wording. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as draft assistance and have a qualified person verify facts, dates, authorities, costs, and final approval language before use. <br>
Risk: Drafts may include unsupported estimates, missing business details, or unresolved placeholders if the user has not supplied complete source information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep missing data outside the final body for user confirmation, and use the included review checklist or prose-lint script where appropriate before relying on the text. <br>
